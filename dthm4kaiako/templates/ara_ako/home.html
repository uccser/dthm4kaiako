Ara Ako is all about developing DT & HM learning resources for you and others to use based on local curriculum kaupapa and contexts. Work in small teams to dive deeper into DT & HM concepts while developing a project-based programme of learning or create supporting resources for teachers new to DT & HM.

We will provide:

  • An incredible atmosphere of fun and creativity
  • Knowledgable DT & HM advisors and support for groups
  • High class facilities for group work including breakout spaces
  • A lot of scrumptious food
  • A few surprises along the way

All educators with a drive to create are invited to apply to attend these events. We’re aiming to bring together a range of attendees based on teaching medium, region, and year levels. It doesn’t matter if you’ve been in education for one year or a lifetime, what is important is that you are ready to create for the community.
